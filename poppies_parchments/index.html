What is Poppies Parchments?

Poppies Parchments is a handmade greeting card boutique.

I believe happiness is always homemade and heartfelt. I started this company because I wanted to share the joy of creating beautiful cards with you (and a few other surprises. You never know what you will find!).

Getting a card from someone always makes you feel good - a beautiful handmade one even more so!

I didn't want to stop the good feeling with just selling cards, though. That's when I decided to have classes to teach different techniques so you, too, can come up with your own unique designs.

Spread the love! Plus, being creative in a group is always more fun! So, come on by - I can’t wait to meet you!
